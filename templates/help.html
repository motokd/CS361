Help Page




About


This program is used to help plan for your tasks online. It could be personal tasks, tasks for school, or business related tasks. Due dates help remind you of when the task is due so that you wont forget.



Guide

• Click on "Add Task" either in the navigation window or the quicklinks guide to add a new task.

• To add a task fill out all the applicable information. This is the task title, priority, and due date. Task details are not required. If information is not entered correctly, you will be notified.

• If adding the task is successful, you will be redirected to the home page where you can see the task and its details.

• From the home page, you can click on a task title and edit or delete the task.
